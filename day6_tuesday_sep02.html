Culture & Shopping Guide

Constitution Hill
Former prison complex where Nelson Mandela was held, now home to the Constitutional Court. A powerful journey through South African history.
Rosebank Art Market
Perfect for authentic African crafts, art, and jewelry. Don't forget to bargain - it's part of the experience and expected!
Marble Restaurant
Award-winning venue famous for live-fire cooking. Request the terrace for stunning sunset views over Johannesburg!
Last-Minute Shopping
Rosebank Market for unique crafts and art. For international brands and luxury items, Sandton City is your best bet.
Spa & Relaxation
If you need to unwind before dinner, Sandton offers excellent spa facilities with 90-minute relaxation packages.
Perfect Photo Moments
Constitution Hill's architecture is stunning. Marble's rooftop terrace offers the best sunset shots of the city skyline.
