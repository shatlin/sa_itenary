✈️ Departure Day Tips

⏰ Airport Timing
International flights: arrive 3 hours early. Domestic: 2 hours. OR Tambo can be busy!
🚗 Transport Options
Drive: 30-45 min from Sandton. Gautrain: 15 min, runs every 12-20 minutes.
🛍️ Last-Minute Gifts
Duty-free has biltong, wine, and crafts. Prices similar to city shops.
💼 Packing Tips
Check airline baggage limits. Keep souvenirs accessible for customs declaration.
💳 VAT Refund
If you spent R250+ on goods, claim VAT refund at airport before check-in.
🍴 Airport Dining
Good restaurants after security: Mugg & Bean, Ocean Basket, Wimpy.
