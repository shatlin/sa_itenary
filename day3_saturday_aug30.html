Pro Route Tips

Must-Do Roads

  • S114: The rhino road - drive slowly along dry riverbed
  • H3: The legendary cat road - everyone sees something here
  • S28: Less busy but incredibly productive for rare sightings

Secret Spots

  • Vurhami Loop: If you have 30 extra minutes - often cheetahs
  • Duke's Water Hole: Turn-off on S114 - usually quiet with good sightings
  • Nkumbe View Site: On H3 - panoramic views, often raptors

Expert Safari Tips

Technology
Download "Latest Sightings" app before entering. No signal in most of park. Take car charger.
Spotting Tricks
Look for: Birds circling (kill), cars stopped (sighting), animals looking in one direction (predator nearby).
Driving Strategy
20-30 km/h on S roads for best sightings. Stop at every water source. Turn engine off at sightings.
Photography
Bean bag for camera stability. Shoot from driver's side when alone. Binoculars essential!
Big 5 Timing
Lions: 06:00-08:00. Leopards: Check trees on H3. Elephants: Afternoon at water. Rhinos: S114 early. Buffalo: Anywhere!
Golden Rule
First 2 hours = 50% of sightings. Be at gate at 06:00! Every minute after sunrise reduces cat sightings.
